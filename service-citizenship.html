Canadian Citizenship Applications, PR Card Renewals & Urgent Processing

About This Service

Transition from permanent resident to Canadian citizen, or maintain your permanent residence status with PR card renewals and travel document applications.

Services We Provide

  • Canadian Citizenship Applications: For permanent residents eligible for citizenship
  • PR Card Renewals: Renewing expired or expiring PR cards
  • Urgent Processing Requests: For citizenship or PR cards
  • Permanent Resident Travel Documents: For PRs abroad without valid PR card
  • Proof of Citizenship: Citizenship certificates
  • Retention of Citizenship: For those born abroad to Canadian parents
  • Residency Obligation Analysis: Assessing compliance with PR requirements

Canadian Citizenship

Eligibility Requirements

  • Permanent Resident Status: Must be a PR
  • Physical Presence: At least 1,095 days (3 years) in Canada during the 5 years before application
  • Tax Filing: Filed taxes for at least 3 years (if required)
  • Language Proficiency: Adequate knowledge of English or French (ages 18-54)
  • Knowledge of Canada: Pass citizenship test (ages 18-54)
  • No Prohibitions: No criminal prohibitions or immigration violations

Physical Presence Calculation

  • Days as PR count as full days
  • Days as temporary resident before becoming PR count as half days (max 365 days)
  • Must be physically present at least 183 days per year in 4 of the last 6 years

Citizenship Test & Interview

  • 20 multiple-choice questions about Canada
  • Must score 15/20 (75%) to pass
  • Tests knowledge of Canadian history, geography, government, laws, and symbols
  • Study guide: Discover Canada

Oath of Citizenship

  • Final step to become a Canadian citizen
  • Attend ceremony (in-person or virtual)
  • Receive citizenship certificate

PR Card Renewal

When to Apply

  • PR card expires every 5 years
  • Apply for renewal 9 months before expiry (or within 9 months after expiry)
  • Can still enter Canada as PR without valid card (but need proof of PR status)

Residency Obligation

To maintain PR status, must meet one of:

  • Physically present in Canada for at least 730 days (2 years) in last 5 years
  • Accompanying Canadian citizen spouse/partner abroad
  • Working for Canadian business or government abroad
  • Accompanying PR spouse/partner working for Canadian business abroad

If Not Meeting Residency Obligation

  • May lose PR status
  • Can provide humanitarian and compassionate reasons
  • Right to appeal if PR card renewal refused
  • We can assess your situation and advise on options

Permanent Resident Travel Document (PRTD)

For permanent residents outside Canada without a valid PR card:

  • Allows PR to return to Canada
  • Apply from outside Canada at visa office
  • Single-entry document
  • Must still meet residency obligation

Urgent Processing

Available in limited circumstances:

Citizenship Urgent Processing

  • Medical reasons
  • Work-related situations
  • Family emergencies
  • Requires supporting documentation

PR Card Urgent Processing

  • Travel for work (proof required)
  • Medical emergency abroad (proof required)
  • Death of family member abroad
  • Pick up urgent PR card in person if approved

Common Issues We Address

  • Physical presence calculation and documentation
  • Gaps in residency or travel history
  • Residency obligation shortfalls
  • Criminal prohibitions and citizenship eligibility
  • Language and citizenship test preparation
  • Lost or damaged PR cards
  • Name changes and document updates
  • Urgent processing justifications

Proof of Citizenship

  • For Canadian citizens by birth or naturalization
  • Citizenship certificate
  • Search of citizenship records
  • Required for passport applications

How We Help

  • Calculate physical presence and residency obligation compliance
  • Assess eligibility for citizenship or PR card renewal
  • Prepare comprehensive applications with supporting documentation
  • Address residency shortfalls with H&C considerations
  • Guide citizenship test preparation
  • Handle urgent processing requests
  • PRTD applications for PRs abroad
  • Respond to procedural fairness letters
  • Appeals for refused PR cards (residency obligation)

Processing Times

  • Citizenship: 12-18 months
  • PR Card Renewal: 2-3 months
  • PR Card Urgent Processing: Days to weeks (if approved)
  • PRTD: 2-4 weeks
  • Proof of Citizenship: 3-5 months
