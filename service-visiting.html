✈ Visiting Canada

Temporary Resident Visas, Super Visas, Extensions & More

About This Service

Whether you're planning to visit Canada for tourism, to visit family, or for business purposes, we help you navigate the temporary resident visa process with confidence.

Services We Provide

  • Temporary Resident Visa (TRV) / Visitor Visa: Applications for tourism, family visits, or short-term stays
  • Super Visa: Multi-year visas for parents and grandparents of Canadian citizens and permanent residents
  • Visitor Record / Extension of Stay: Extending your authorized stay in Canada
  • Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA): For visa-exempt nationals traveling by air
  • Business Visitor Applications: For legitimate business activities without entering the Canadian labor market

Eligibility Requirements

To visit Canada as a temporary resident, you must demonstrate:

  • Valid travel document (passport)
  • Sufficient financial means to support yourself during your stay
  • Intention to leave Canada at the end of your authorized stay
  • No criminal inadmissibility
  • Good health (medical exam may be required)
  • Strong ties to your home country

Super Visa Requirements

For parents and grandparents seeking extended visits:

  • Letter of invitation from child or grandchild in Canada (citizen or PR)
  • Proof of relationship
  • Canadian host must meet minimum income requirements
  • Medical insurance coverage of at least $100,000 for at least one year
  • Medical examination

How We Help

  • Assess your eligibility and advise on the appropriate visa type
  • Prepare comprehensive application packages with supporting documentation
  • Draft persuasive letters of explanation and invitation letters
  • Guide you on financial documentation requirements
  • Assist with medical insurance arrangements for Super Visas
  • Handle extensions and visitor record applications
  • Respond to procedural fairness letters or additional document requests

Processing Times

Processing times vary by country and visa office. Typical ranges:

  • Visitor Visa: 2-8 weeks (varies significantly by country)
  • Super Visa: 2-3 months
  • Extensions: 4-6 months (apply at least 30 days before expiry)
