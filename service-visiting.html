Professional Support for Visitor Visas, Super Visas & Temporary Resident Applications

Canada welcomes millions of visitors every year for tourism, family visits, and business travel. However, approval for a visitor application is never guaranteed. Each application is assessed individually by immigration officers, and even small errors or weak documentation can result in refusal.

We provide professional support for individuals seeking to visit Canada temporarily, ensuring applications are properly prepared, clearly presented, and aligned with immigration requirements.

Visitor & Temporary Resident Services

Depending on your nationality, travel history, and purpose of travel, different authorization may be required. We assist with:

  • Visitor Visa (Temporary Resident Visa â€“ TRV)
    For tourism, family visits, or short-term stays in Canada
  • Super Visa
    Long-term visit options for eligible parents and grandparents of Canadian citizens or permanent residents
  • Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA)
    For visa-exempt nationals travelling to Canada by air
  • Visitor Record / Extension of Stay
    For individuals already in Canada who wish to extend their authorized stay
  • Business Visitor Applications
    For qualifying business activities that do not involve entering the Canadian labour market

How We Support You

Every application is different. Our role is to ensure your case is presented accurately and strategically. We assist by:

  • Assessing your situation and recommending the appropriate application type
  • Preparing and reviewing application forms and supporting documentation
  • Drafting clear and effective explanation letters where required
  • Identifying and addressing potential risk factors before submission
  • Advising on next steps following a refusal, including re-application strategies where appropriate

Previous Refusals

A refusal does not necessarily mean the end of your plans. We review refusal decisions, identify the reasons provided by immigration officers, and advise on the most appropriate way forward based on your circumstances.
