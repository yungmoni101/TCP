Express Entry, Provincial Nominees & Quebec Immigration

About This Service

Permanent residence in Canada offers the right to live, work, and study anywhere in Canada. We guide you through Express Entry, Provincial Nominee Programs, and Quebec immigration pathways.

Services We Provide

  • Express Entry: Federal Skilled Worker, Canadian Experience Class, Federal Skilled Trades
  • Provincial Nominee Programs (PNP): All provinces and territories
  • Quebec Immigration: Quebec Skilled Worker Program and others
  • Atlantic Immigration Program: For Atlantic provinces
  • Rural and Northern Immigration Pilot: Community-driven immigration
  • Agri-Food Pilot: For agricultural workers
  • CRS Score Improvement Strategies: Maximizing your Express Entry profile

Express Entry System

Express Entry manages applications for three federal programs:

Federal Skilled Worker (FSW)

  • For skilled workers with foreign work experience
  • Minimum 1 year of continuous full-time skilled work experience (NOC 0, A, or B)
  • Language proficiency: CLB 7 in English or French
  • Educational credential assessment required
  • Must score 67+ points on FSW selection grid

Canadian Experience Class (CEC)

  • For workers with Canadian work experience
  • Minimum 1 year of skilled Canadian work experience in past 3 years
  • Language proficiency: CLB 7 for NOC 0/A, CLB 5 for NOC B
  • Plan to live outside Quebec

Federal Skilled Trades (FST)

  • For skilled tradespeople
  • Minimum 2 years of full-time work experience in eligible trade
  • Valid job offer or certificate of qualification
  • Language proficiency: CLB 5 for speaking/listening, CLB 4 for reading/writing

Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS)

Express Entry candidates are ranked based on:

  • Age (maximum 110 points)
  • Education (maximum 150 points)
  • Language proficiency (maximum 160 points)
  • Canadian work experience (maximum 80 points)
  • Additional factors: spouse points, Canadian education, siblings, French proficiency, provincial nomination (+600 points)

Provincial Nominee Programs

Every province and territory (except Quebec) operates PNPs:

  • Alberta Immigrant Nominee Program (AINP)
  • British Columbia PNP (BC PNP)
  • Ontario Immigrant Nominee Program (OINP)
  • Saskatchewan Immigrant Nominee Program (SINP)
  • Manitoba Provincial Nominee Program (MPNP)
  • And more...

Provincial nomination adds 600 CRS points, essentially guaranteeing an invitation to apply.
