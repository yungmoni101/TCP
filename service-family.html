Spousal Sponsorship, Dependent Children & Parent/Grandparent Sponsorship

About This Service

Reunite with your loved ones in Canada. We assist with spousal sponsorship, dependent children sponsorship, parent and grandparent sponsorship, and other family class applications.

Services We Provide

  • Spousal Sponsorship (Inside Canada): For spouses/common-law partners in Canada
  • Spousal Sponsorship (Outside Canada): For spouses/common-law partners abroad
  • Dependent Children Sponsorship: For dependent sons and daughters
  • Parent & Grandparent Sponsorship: Through the PGP program
  • Adoption: Sponsorship of adopted children
  • Other Relatives: In limited circumstances
  • Relationship Evidence Preparation: Building strong proof of genuine relationships

Spousal & Common-Law Partner Sponsorship

Sponsor Eligibility

  • Canadian citizen or permanent resident
  • At least 18 years old
  • Not receiving social assistance (except for disability)
  • No criminal convictions for violent crimes or family-related offenses
  • Not in default of previous sponsorship undertakings
  • Able to provide basic needs for sponsored person

Relationship Requirements

  • Married: Legal marriage recognized in country where performed and under Canadian law
  • Common-Law: Living together continuously for at least 12 months
  • Conjugal Partner: In relationship for at least 12 months but unable to live together or marry due to barriers

Inside vs. Outside Canada Applications

  • Inside Canada (Inland): Sponsored person must be in Canada; can apply for open work permit while waiting; cannot appeal if refused
  • Outside Canada (Outland): Sponsored person can be in Canada or abroad; faster processing; right of appeal if refused

Parent & Grandparent Sponsorship

Program Requirements

  • Random selection process - must submit interest to sponsor form when intake opens
  • Selected sponsors invited to submit full application
  • Must meet Minimum Necessary Income (MNI) for past 3 tax years
  • Income requirements increase based on household size
  • Undertaking: 20-year commitment to provide financial support

Alternative: Super Visa

If PGP not available or while waiting:

  • Multiple-entry visa valid up to 10 years
  • Allows stays of up to 2 years per entry
  • Requires proof of medical insurance ($100,000 minimum)
  • No income test for sponsor (but must show ability to support)

Dependent Children Sponsorship

Sponsor your dependent children:

  • Children under 22 years old who are not married or in common-law relationship
  • Children 22+ who have depended on parents financially since before age 22 due to mental or physical condition
  • Different process for adopted children

Key Documentation Requirements

For All Sponsorships

  • Proof of sponsor's status in Canada
  • Financial documents
  • Police certificates
  • Medical examinations
  • Identity documents

For Spousal Sponsorship

  • Marriage certificate or proof of common-law relationship
  • Photos together throughout relationship
  • Communication records (messages, calls, emails)
  • Joint financial documents
  • Proof of cohabitation (lease, bills, mail)
  • Letters from friends and family
  • Relationship timeline and history

Common Challenges We Address

  • Proving genuineness of relationship for short courtships
  • Age differences between partners
  • Previous marriages and divorces
  • Cultural marriage traditions
  • Limited documentation due to pandemic or circumstances
  • Language barriers
  • Sponsor's previous immigration violations or criminal record

How We Help

  • Assess eligibility of sponsor and sponsored person
  • Advise on inland vs. outland applications
  • Prepare comprehensive applications with strong relationship evidence
  • Guide document collection and proof of relationship
  • Calculate and verify income requirements
  • Prepare persuasive relationship statements and timelines
  • Handle interviews and additional document requests
  • Address concerns about genuineness of relationship
  • Respond to procedural fairness letters

Processing Times

  • Spousal Sponsorship (Inside Canada): 12 months
  • Spousal Sponsorship (Outside Canada): 12 months
  • Parent & Grandparent Sponsorship: 20-24 months (after invitation)
  • Dependent Children: 8-12 months
