♥ Humanitarian & Compassionate Applications

Exceptional Circumstances & Discretionary Considerations

Humanitarian and Compassionate (H&C) applications are intended for individuals who do not qualify under standard immigration programs but face compelling personal circumstances. These applications are discretionary and require careful analysis, strong supporting documentation, and well-prepared written submissions.

We assist clients in assessing whether an H&C application may be appropriate and in preparing thoughtful, well-documented applications that clearly present their circumstances.

Humanitarian & Compassionate Services

We provide guidance and application support for H&C matters, including:

  • H&C Applications for Permanent Residence
    For individuals seeking permanent residence based on exceptional humanitarian considerations
  • Best Interests of a Child Considerations
    Where children may be significantly affected by an immigration decision
  • Establishment in Canada
    Assessment of long-term residence, ties, and integration in Canada
  • Hardship & Exceptional Circumstances
    Including personal, family, or humanitarian factors
  • H&C Considerations for Status Matters
    Including certain permanent resident residency obligation situations

Understanding H&C Applications

H&C applications are assessed on a case-by-case basis. Immigration officers consider whether requiring an applicant to leave Canada or apply through standard channels would result in hardship that goes beyond what is normally expected. These applications are not routine and require a clear, well-supported explanation of the circumstances involved.

A Careful and Strategic Process

H&C cases often involve sensitive personal situations and heightened scrutiny. Success depends on how clearly the facts are presented, how well the evidence supports the request, and whether the application addresses both positive and challenging aspects of the case.

We focus on helping clients understand the risks, limitations, and expectations associated with H&C applications before proceeding.

How We Support You

Humanitarian and Compassionate applications require a thoughtful and structured approach. We assist by:

  • Assessing whether an H&C application is appropriate for your circumstances
  • Identifying relevant humanitarian factors that may apply
  • Advising on evidence and documentation needed to support the application
  • Preparing detailed written submissions that clearly explain the circumstances
  • Assisting with related applications where appropriate
  • Supporting clients through requests for additional information or concerns raised by immigration authorities

Important Considerations

H&C applications are discretionary and can take significant time to process. Not every situation qualifies, and outcomes cannot be guaranteed. We believe in providing honest, realistic guidance so clients can make informed decisions before proceeding.
