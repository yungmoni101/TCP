Start-Up Visa, Entrepreneurship & Provincial Programs

Canada offers several immigration pathways for entrepreneurs, investors, and self-employed individuals who can contribute to the Canadian economy. These pathways are highly structured and carefully assessed. Business ownership, investment, or property purchase alone does not grant immigration status, and applications must be strategically aligned with recognized immigration programs.

We advise clients exploring business and investment-based immigration options and provide guidance on how business activities or assets may support lawful immigration pathways when properly structured.

Business & Investment Immigration Pathways

We assist clients with a range of federal and provincial business-related programs, including:

  • Start-Up Visa Program
    For entrepreneurs with innovative business ideas supported by designated Canadian organizations
  • Provincial Entrepreneur Programs
    Business immigration pathways offered by provinces and territories for business owners and investors
  • Self-Employed Persons Program
    For individuals with qualifying experience in cultural, athletic, or farming activities
  • Owner-Operator & Business-Based Work Permits
    For business owners seeking temporary work authorization in Canada
  • Intra-Company Transfers (ICT)
    For executives, managers, and key personnel transferring to a Canadian branch
  • Quebec Business Immigration Programs
    Business and self-employment pathways under QuÃ©bec's distinct immigration system

A Strategic Approach to Business Immigration

Business and investment immigration requires more than capital. Applications are assessed on factors such as business viability, economic benefit, management involvement, and compliance with program requirements. We help clients understand which pathways may be realistic and appropriate based on their background, objectives, and risk tolerance.

Where permanent residence is not immediately available, we also advise on step-by-step strategies that may begin with temporary work authorization and progress toward permanent residence over time.

How We Support You

Business immigration applications involve detailed planning and careful documentation. We assist by:

  • Assessing eligibility across available business and investment programs
  • Advising on suitable immigration strategies based on your profile and goals
  • Reviewing and supporting business plans used for immigration purposes
  • Guiding preparation of financial and source-of-funds documentation
  • Preparing and reviewing immigration applications and supporting materials
  • Advising on compliance obligations after approval
  • Assisting with applications following refusals or reassessments, where applicable

Important Considerations

Business and investment pathways involve heightened scrutiny by immigration authorities. We help clients avoid common risks such as unrealistic business proposals, insufficient documentation, or pursuing programs that do not align with their circumstances.
