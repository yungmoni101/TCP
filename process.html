How We Work

Simple, Strategic, Successful

Our proven process ensures your application receives professional attention at every step

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with a detailed consultation to understand your background, goals, and circumstances. We'll assess your eligibility for various immigration programs and provide honest, realistic guidance on your options.

Duration: 30-60 minutes | Fee: Consultation fee applies

2

Strategic Planning

Based on your consultation, we develop a personalized immigration strategy. We'll recommend the most suitable pathway, outline required documentation, provide a timeline, and present our fee structure for the services needed.

Outcome: Clear roadmap and service agreement

3

Document Collection & Preparation

We guide you through gathering all required documents‚Äîeducation credentials, work experience letters, police certificates, medical exams, financial documents, and more. We review each document for completeness and compliance with IRCC requirements.

Timeline: Varies by case (typically 2-8 weeks)

4

Application Preparation

Our licensed consultants prepare your application forms, supporting letters, and documentation packages. Peter Poon personally reviews every application to ensure accuracy, completeness, and strategic presentation.

Quality assurance: Every application reviewed by RCIC

5

Submission & Monitoring

We submit your application to IRCC and provide you with confirmation. We monitor your application throughout the process, respond to any requests from IRCC, and keep you informed of progress and developments.

Communication: Regular updates on application status

6

Decision & Next Steps

When IRCC makes a decision, we review it with you and guide you on next steps‚Äîwhether preparing for landing, activating your visa, or addressing any conditions. We're here to support you even after approval.

Support: Guidance through final steps and landing
